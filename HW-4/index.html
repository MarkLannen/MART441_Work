Choose Your Own Adventure through
the Bob Marshall Wilderness!

It's summer in Montanan and you've got a week off from work. Two old friends have come to see you in Montana to visit and they want you to take them out in to the Wilderness.

You decide to take them to the Bob Marshall and enter at the Benchmark trailhead. This will show them something really wild!

At the trailhead, you load up your gear and look at the map. Now you've got some decisions to make!

Do you and your crew want to camp along the Sun River the first night or make it all the way to a hiden lake?
